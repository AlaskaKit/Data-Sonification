Data Sonification Project is a web service for converting data (specifically, time series, e.g. exchange rates or daily temperature) into sound. Made for studying purposes.

Pick an excel (*.xls, *.xlsx), or *.csv file, set up sample duration (from 10 to 999 sec) click "Submit" and wait until you will be offered to save an output *.wav file.

Attention! This is a prototype. The process can take up to 10 min depending on sample duration.

Do not close the page or take other actions on the page until you are offered to save or open the file!

For detailed instructions and requirements for the input files and data formats visit

github.com/AlaskaKit/Data-Sonification.
